# Delta Review Policy

## Daily review

Purpose:

- Record learning results.
- Compare actual learning with planned operations.
- Extract weak points and next review candidates.
- Adjust the next short-term operations when needed.

Inputs:

- learning history
- active operations
- quiz score or comprehension
- weak points

## Weekly review

Purpose:

- Check progress against the exam plan.
- Rebalance subjects and weak areas.
- Rebuild next week operations.

## Monthly review

Purpose:

- Review roadmap and plan assumptions.
- Incorporate mock exam or past paper results.
- Adjust strategy toward the 2026-08-23 exam date.

## Rule

Delta review is a learning review, not a development review.
